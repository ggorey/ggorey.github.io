Welcome to Grace

For ENGL3844, we were asked to design a website. Well, welcome to that website. Throughout the course of the semester, we've attempted doing some research on how education has impacted our lives. I wrote a few blogs on the topics (which you can find below) and with the help of my group, have compiled quite the collection of educational material. Below is a closer look into education. Please be sure to check out our podcast as well as a nice little video we put together for you! Happy exploring!!

First Medium Post

Click here to read my article. In this medium article, I tried to reach out to an audience that didn't necessarily have access to higher education like university. Many of us simply associate education with a classic old brick building where an old lady is yelling at you with a ruler in her hand. BUT there's so much more to it than that. Surprisingly enough, Netflix serves as a great tool in helping others become more educated on topics that may not have been previously heard of. Documentaries. It's all about the documentaries, man.
